Cookie Policy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, mobile phone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. These files allow the website to remember your activities and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to reconfigure them every time you visit the site.

2. What types of cookies do we use?

We use different types of cookies on our website to improve your browsing experience. Below, we explain the different types of cookies we use and their purposes:

Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse our website and use its basic features. They cannot be disabled in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions taken by you, such as setting privacy preferences or logging in.

  • Purpose: Ensure the correct functioning of the website, maintain the user session, and perform security functions.

    • Performance Cookies

    These cookies allow us to analyze how users interact with our website, such as the pages they visit most frequently and if they encounter any error messages. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.

  • Purpose: Improve the website's functionality and user experience.

    • Functional Cookies

    These cookies allow the website to remember your choices and preferences (e.g., the language you prefer or the region you're in) to provide you with a more personalized experience.

  • Purpose: Provide a more customized browsing experience.

    • Advertising or Marketing Cookies

    These cookies are used to show relevant ads to you, both on and off our website. They also help us measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

  • Purpose: Manage advertisements and personalize ads based on your behavior and preferences.

    • Social Media Cookies

    These cookies allow you to share content on social networks such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and others. Social media cookies may collect information about your online activity to provide you with personalized advertising on other platforms.

  • Purpose: Facilitate social media integration and personalize ads.

    • 3. How can you control and manage cookies?

    When you first access our website, we will ask you to accept or reject the use of cookies. If you choose to accept them, you can change your preference at any time. If you prefer not to receive cookies, you can configure your browser to block them or to alert you whenever an attempt is made to store a cookie.

    Options to manage cookies in your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Go to "Settings" → "Privacy and security" → "Cookies and other site data" → "Manage cookies".
  • Mozilla Firefox: Go to "Options" → "Privacy & Security" → "Cookies and Site Data".
  • Safari: Go to "Preferences" → "Privacy" → "Cookies and website data".
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to "Settings" → "Privacy, search, and services" → "Cookies".

    • Please note that if you disable certain cookies, some features of our website may be affected.

    4. Third-party cookies

    We may use third-party services that also set cookies on your device when you browse our website. Some examples are web analytics providers or social media platforms. These third-party cookies are used to improve advertising, personalize content, and analyze website traffic.

    Service providers like Google Analytics, Facebook, and others may use their own cookies to collect browsing data and display personalized ads. We recommend reviewing their privacy and cookie policies.

    5. How do we use the information collected by cookies?

    The information we collect through cookies is used to improve your browsing experience on our website, personalize the content you see, optimize site performance, and show you relevant advertisements. We also use it to conduct analysis on site traffic and user interaction with our content.

    6. How long are cookies stored?

    The duration of cookies may vary depending on the type of cookie. Some cookies, like necessary cookies, are deleted automatically when you close your browser, while others may remain on your device for a longer period. The duration is usually specified when the cookie is set, and you can manage the deletion of cookies at any time through your browser settings.
