Hyundai i30 N

The Hyundai i30 N is a hot hatch that combines thrilling performance with everyday practicality. Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, the i30 N delivers 280 horsepower and 392 Nm of torque. With this power, it accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in just 6.1 seconds, offering an exciting driving experience both on the road and the track.

Designed with performance in mind, the Hyundai i30 N features a sporty exterior with aggressive styling, a wide front grille, and dual exhausts. The front and rear suspension systems are tuned to enhance handling, while the limited-slip differential ensures maximum grip during high-speed corners. The car’s aerodynamic features help improve stability and downforce, making it a highly capable hatchback on twisty roads or race circuits.

Inside, the Hyundai i30 N combines comfort and sportiness with a driver-focused cockpit. Sports seats, a leather-wrapped steering wheel, and advanced infotainment systems enhance the driving experience, while still offering the practicality and comfort needed for everyday use.

Key Features

Engine: 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 280 horsepower

Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 6.1 seconds

Top Speed: 155 mph

Handling: Tuned suspension with limited-slip differential for improved cornering

Interior: Driver-focused cockpit with sports seats and advanced infotainment system

The Hyundai i30 N offers a perfect balance of performance and everyday usability, making it the ideal choice for those who want excitement without compromise.
