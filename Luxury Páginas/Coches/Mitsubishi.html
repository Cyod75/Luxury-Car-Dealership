Mitsubishi ASX 2024

Performance and Efficiency

The Mitsubishi ASX 2024 is the perfect blend of compact SUV versatility and efficient performance. Powered by a range of turbocharged petrol and hybrid engine options, the ASX 2024 delivers an optimal balance between power and fuel efficiency. The 1.5-liter petrol engine offers a smooth drive with 150 horsepower, while the hybrid variant enhances performance with lower emissions and improved fuel economy.

Design and Comfort

Designed to meet the demands of modern drivers, the 2024 ASX features a sleek and bold exterior. The refreshed front grille, LED headlights, and dynamic body lines give it a fresh, sporty appearance. Inside, the cabin is crafted for comfort, featuring a spacious layout, premium materials, and an intuitive infotainment system. The ASX 2024 can comfortably seat five passengers, with plenty of cargo space for all your gear.

Technology and Safety Features

With a suite of advanced technology, the 2024 ASX ensures an enhanced driving experience. It comes equipped with a large touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a premium sound system. The vehicle also offers a variety of driver-assistance systems, including lane-keeping assist, forward collision mitigation, and adaptive cruise control to provide peace of mind on every journey.

Key Features

  • Choice of petrol or hybrid engine options
  • 150 horsepower from the 1.5L petrol engine
  • Fuel-efficient hybrid variant for better economy and lower emissions
  • Dynamic and modern exterior design with LED lights and updated grille
  • Spacious and comfortable interior with premium materials
  • Advanced technology including Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a large touchscreen
  • Comprehensive safety features such as lane-keeping assist and adaptive cruise control

The Mitsubishi ASX 2024 is the ideal compact SUV for those who want a stylish, efficient, and technologically advanced vehicle for everyday use and weekend adventures.

Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution

Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution: A True Performance Legend

Performance and Power

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution is an icon in the world of high-performance sedans. Powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, the Evo delivers an impressive 291 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse engine is paired with a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system, ensuring exceptional handling and traction in any driving condition. The Lancer Evolution can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.1 seconds, providing an exhilarating driving experience.

Design and Handling

With its aggressive stance, wide body, and sporty lines, the Lancer Evolution looks as fast as it feels. The functional aerodynamics, including the large front splitter and rear spoiler, help improve stability and downforce at high speeds. The Evo's lightweight chassis and finely-tuned suspension system make it a true track performer, capable of handling corners with precision and ease.

Interior and Technology

Inside, the Lancer Evolution offers a driver-focused cockpit, featuring supportive sports seats, a straightforward dashboard, and an easy-to-read instrument cluster. While the interior is minimalistic, it’s designed for driving enthusiasts, with features like a center-mounted tachometer and a performance-oriented infotainment system. While not luxurious, the cabin provides all the necessary tools to focus on the road.

Key Features

  • 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 291 horsepower
  • All-wheel-drive system for improved handling and traction
  • 0-60 mph in just 5.1 seconds
  • Aggressive exterior design with functional aerodynamics
  • Track-ready suspension system for optimal performance
  • Driver-focused interior with sports seats and performance-oriented features

The Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution remains a favorite among driving enthusiasts, offering a blend of power, handling, and everyday practicality in a performance sedan.
